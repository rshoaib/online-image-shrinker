Memes are the language of the internet. Whether it's a reaction image, a cat photo, or a deep-fried shitpost, memes convey complex emotions in a single glance.

## The Classic Formula

While meme formats evolve daily, one style remains eternal: **Top Text, Bottom Text**.
*   **Font**: Impact (Bold, Condensed).
*   **Style**: White text with a thick black outline.
*   **Vibe**: Funny, relatable, or ironic.
