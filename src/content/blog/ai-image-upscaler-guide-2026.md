## What Is AI Image Upscaling?

Traditional image resizing simply stretches pixels. A 200├ù200 image stretched to 800├ù800 becomes a blurry, pixelated mess. Every pixel is just copied four times.

**AI upscaling is different.** Instead of copying pixels, AI models trained on millions of images *predict* what the missing detail should look like. They fill in textures, sharpen edges, reconstruct faces, and add realistic detail that was never in the original file.

The result: a genuinely higher-resolution image that looks sharp and natural ÔÇö not stretched.

## How Does It Work? (Plain English)

AI upscalers use deep neural networks ÔÇö specifically a type called **Super-Resolution CNNs** (Convolutional Neural Networks) or **Diffusion Models**. Here is the simplified version:

1. **Training:** The AI is shown millions of high-resolution images alongside their deliberately degraded low-resolution versions.
2. **Learning:** The AI learns patterns ÔÇö how hair strands look at high resolution, how fabric textures should appear, how text edges become crisp.
3. **Predicting:** When you give it a new low-res image, it applies those learned patterns to "hallucinate" (reconstruct) realistic high-resolution detail.

**Think of it like this:** If you showed someone a blurry photo of a cat, they could imagine the fur texture, the whisker details, and the eye reflections. AI upscalers do the same thing ÔÇö but mathematically.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Over-upscaling.** Going from 200├ù200 straight to 8├ù (1600├ù1600) will produce AI-generated artifacts. **Use 2├ù or 4├ù for the best results.** Only use 8├ù on images that start at decent quality.

2. **Ignoring the source format.** Heavily compressed JPEGs already have artifacts. Upscaling amplifies those artifacts. If possible, start from a PNG or the original file.

3. **Not compressing after upscaling.** A 4├ù upscaled image can be 10ÔÇô20 MB. Always [compress your upscaled images](/tool/compress) before using them on the web.

4. **Using upscaling for text.** AI may subtly alter letterforms in text-heavy images. For screenshots with text, use 2├ù maximum and verify the text is still readable.

5. **Expecting miracles from terrible sources.** A 50├ù50 pixel avatar upscaled to 400├ù400 will look "AI-generated" ÔÇö the model is inventing 98% of the pixel data. Be realistic about what AI can recover.

**Is AI upscaling the same as resizing?**
