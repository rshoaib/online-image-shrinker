## What Is a QR Code?

A **QR code** (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that stores data ÔÇö usually a URL, text, or contact information. Any smartphone camera can scan it instantly.

Unlike traditional barcodes that hold 20ÔÇô25 characters, QR codes can store **up to 4,296 alphanumeric characters**, enough for a full URL, paragraph of text, or Wi-Fi credentials.

## QR Code Best Practices

Follow these rules to ensure your codes work reliably:

### Sizing for Print
- **Minimum size:** 2 cm ├ù 2 cm (0.8 in ├ù 0.8 in) for close-range scanning
- **Billboards:** Scale up proportionally ÔÇö at least 25 cm for 3-meter viewing distance
- **Business cards:** 2.5 cm ├ù 2.5 cm works well

### Color Contrast
- **Do:** Dark foreground on light background
- **DonÔÇÖt:** Light foreground on dark background (many scanners struggle with this)
- **DonÔÇÖt:** Use red/green combinations (colorblind users canÔÇÖt distinguish them)

### Testing
