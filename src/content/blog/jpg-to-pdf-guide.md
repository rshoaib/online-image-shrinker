Sending multiple image files via email is unprofessional and messy. Whether you are submitting a homework assignment, sending receipts to your accountant, or building a portfolio, combining images into a single PDF is the standard way to go.

## Why convert to PDF?

*   **Universal:** a PDF looks the same on every device (Phone, Mac, Windows).
*   **Compact:** One file is easier to manage than 20 separate JPGs.
*   **Professional:** It shows you are organized.
