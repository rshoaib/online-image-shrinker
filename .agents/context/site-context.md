# OnlineImageShrinker — Site Context

> **URL**: https://onlineimageshrinker.com
> **Stack**: Vite-React SPA + Vercel
> **Revenue**: AdSense
> **Core Value**: 28+ free browser-based image tools — compress, resize, crop, remove backgrounds, and more. No upload to servers, 100% client-side.

---

## 🎤 Brand Voice

- **Tone**: Friendly, practical, tool-first. "Compress your image to under 100KB — free, in 2 seconds."
- **Style**: Short sentences. Before/after comparisons. Bold file sizes and percentages.
- **Address**: Second person ("you"). Help designers, devs, and everyday users.
- **Differentiator**: Always mention "free, no upload, works in your browser" within the first 100 words.
- **Competitor framing**: Position against Canva (requires signup), TinyPNG (limited free), Adobe (expensive).
